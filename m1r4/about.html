Born in the Caribbean

M1R4 represents the vibrant spirit of Trinidad and Tobago, where fashion meets culture in the most beautiful way. Our designs are inspired by the rich heritage of our twin-island nation.

From the colorful streets of Port of Spain to the serene beaches of Tobago, every piece tells a story of Caribbean excellence and style.

Join our community of fashion enthusiasts who celebrate the unique blend of tradition and modernity that defines Caribbean fashion.
